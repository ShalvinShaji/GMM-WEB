    About us

    The Best medical Solution With 52 Years of Experience

    Rev George Mathen Medical Mission hospital is a not-for-profit, 35-bedded primary health care institution located in Mallappally. The hospital provides professional medical services in General Medicine, Pediatrics, Cardiology, Orthopedics, Emergency Medicine, nephrology, ENT & Community Medicine With its deserved reputation for reliability and concern for the well being of the less fortunate among the community, GMM Hospital has over the years emerged as the prime healing hub of the wider area.

    Departments

    General Medicine

    The department of general medicine deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases.

    Cardiology

    The department of cardiology diagonizes and provides treatment for disorders related to heart and cardiovascular system.

    Pediatrics

    The department provides medical care to the infants, children, adolescents, and young adults.

    Orthopedics

    Provide medical care to issues related to musculoskeletal system and treating injuries to bones, joints, ligaments, or tendons.

    nephrology

    Provides OPD based medical to diseases related to kidney.

    ENT

    Medical specialty which provides treatment to diseases related to Ear, Nose, and Throat. We provide OPD based care.
